The story of us

The First
Date

His birthday · A café · A footpath · A look

Act I

🎂

His birthday. Her half-day. The perfect excuse.

She snuck away from school early. He had no idea how this afternoon would change everything.

Act II

🏃

He walked in. Spotted unfamiliar faces. Panicked.

Her parents? Already here? He did the only logical thing — sprinted to the billing counter to order a birthday cake. Very smooth.

Act III

🎵

Waiting at the counter. A song came on.

It said everything he couldn't. Two people who could feel everything but couldn't say a word.

The song that played at the exact right moment

So close. So much unsaid. So perfectly timed.

Act IV

🚶

Order in hand. He stepped outside.

Mum calling. Mind racing. The afternoon was slipping away.

Act V

🌅

She followed him out. Onto the footpath.

"No one came to pick me up."
Four words. The whole world shifted.

Act VI · The Moment

👁️

No words. Just eye contact.

Phone ringing, cake in hand, heart pounding — and in that single look, he already knew. She was the one he'd truly love and cherish.

" Some love stories begin with grand gestures.
Ours began with a panicked sprint,
a birthday cake, and one look
that said everything words couldn't.

— The footpath, 3 months ago

Happy 3 months, my love.

Here's to every chaotic, beautiful moment ahead ♡
